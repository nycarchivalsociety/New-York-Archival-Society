Support Us

The Municipal Library and Archives preserve and make available the City government's historical records. We need your help to continue preserving the materials and to make our holdings available to the general public. The Archival Society, a non-profit founded in 1976, supports the work of the Municipal Archives through fundraising and services as a conduit for grants and donations. Charitable gifts help support preservation, digitization, public programming and internships. All donations are tax deductible.



Your contributions will help preserve the city's valuable heritage for generations to come.
