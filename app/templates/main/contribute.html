The Municipal Library and Archives preserve and make available City government's historical records. We need your help to continue preservation of the materials and to make our holdings available to the general public. The Archival Society, a non-profit founded in 1976, supports the work of the Municipal Archives through fundraising and serving as a conduit for grants and donations. Charitable gifts help support preservation, digitization, public programming and internships. All donations are tax deductible.
