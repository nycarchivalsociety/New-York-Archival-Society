Proceedings of the Board of Aldermen Collection (1831-1853), 39 vols.

$50/volume

The Proceedings of the Board of Aldermen collection at the Municipal Library, which dates from 1831 - 1937, is an important resource for the history of city legislative proceedings. In particular, the volumes from 1831 - 1853, given their age and use, have suffered considerable damage. Adopting these volumes would allow us to give them new bindings, making them safer and easier for researchers to handle.

Adopted by Robert Davis, Lucie Iuzzolini, Miles Freeman, Brad Reid
