Departament Of Records Public Programs

The NYC Department of Records and Information Services (DORIS) hosts rotating exhibits. Past exhibits include “Ebb & Flow: Tapping into the History of New York City's Water”, “Immigrant Voices”, “Unlikely Historians”, "Women Make History," "In the Shadow of the Highway: Robert Moses and the Battle for Downtown," and "Little Syria". View treasures from the archives and learn about the history of New York City.
