Department Of Records Public Programs

The NYC Department of Records and Information Services (DORIS) hosts rotating exhibits. Past exhibits include:

  • Ebb & Flow-Tapping into the History of New York City's Water
  • Immigrant Voices
  • Unlikely Historians
  • Women Make History
  • In the Shadow of the Highway: Robert Moses and the Battle for Downtown
  • Little Syria

The exhibit 'Uniting the Boroughs' which explores the creation the TriBorough bridge is open through August, 2024.

Open from 9:00AM to 4:00PM, Monday through Friday, except for holidays.
31 Chambers, room 109, NY NY 10007
