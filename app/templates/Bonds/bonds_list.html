Vintage New York City Bonds Now Available for Purchase

The NYC Department of Records and Information Services is offering a rare opportunity to own original, redeemed vintage bonds from the 1920s to the 1980s. These beautifully illustrated documents, signed by notable mayors such as James Walker, Fiorello La Guardia, John Lindsay, and Abraham Beame, hold historical significance. Originally issued to fund essential city projects—including school construction, transit expansion, and water supply improvements—these bonds are now available as unique artifacts of New York City's rich financial and municipal history.
